About

ᴋʏᴀ

ᴋʏᴀ is a Discord.py-project, which allows you to execute multiple nuke-commands through your preferred bot, provided you have its token. This project was launched in May 2021 by two German Developers.

History

  • Saturday, May 22th 2021

    Some progress

    Besides designing the images for our socials, releasing ᴋʏᴀ's YouTube-Channel and beginning to set up a discord server, we finally added all spy-commands and made the console, in which you execute the bot-commands, more colorful.

  • Wednesday, May 19th 1021

    A bit of variety

    Since we gave our project a name, we coded a lot in the last days to put ᴋʏᴀ into effect. So now, it's time to have a bit of variety by designing our logo.

  • Saturday, May 15th 2021

    From Bot to Client

    After careful thinking we came to the decision to develop a Nuke-Client rather than a Nuke-Bot, because a client (app) allows us to design the bot more unnoticeable and therefore much stronger. In addition to that, we named our project "ᴋʏᴀ".

  • Wednesday, May 12th 2021

    The first launch

    Today we, Yaaqez & Liyue decided spontaneous to begin a new, unique and game changing project in the area of Discord-Nuking. So, we avidly started to set up a test-bot and ran the first lines of code.
